History

  • The Toronto Raptors are a professional Canadian basketball team established in 1995.
  • They play at Scotiabank Arena in Toronto, Ontario. Their colors are red, black and white.
  • The franchise has won 1 NBA championship, with the only 1 in 2019 by defeating the Golden State Warriors in 6 games.
